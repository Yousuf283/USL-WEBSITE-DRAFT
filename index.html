What We're About

A league of all degrees in the university with each degree having its own domestic tournament to qualify for the championship! With the best team coming out of each major, we will see which degree prevails in the first edition of the annual University Super League!

When It Takes Place

The qualifiers are scheduled from September 11th to 25th, with the final tournament set to occur right after reading week. To view the complete timetable, click here.

Where It's Held

Matches will be played at Finsbury Leusire Centre EC1V 3PU, only a 10 minute walk away from City University of London.
